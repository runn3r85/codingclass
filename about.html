About Mr. Barrette

Class of 2018

I am a teacher at City Arts and Technology High School.

My interests are teaching coding and mathematics, particularly algebra to the freshmen. I also enjoy running and watching the Green Bay Packers pulverize the Oakland Raiders.

I attended the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire for undergraduate school and majored in Mathematics and Spanish. I attended the University of California, Davis for graduate school for a Masters in mathematics.
